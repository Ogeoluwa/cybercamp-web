My favourite food is Fried Rice

its very nice and easy to make

Ingredients needed for Fried rice

  • Cooked rice
  • Chopped vegetables
  • Chicken
  • Liver and beef pieces

How do you make Fried rice?

  1. Wash and spice the chicken pieces. Boil until tender, then fry or grill them.
  2. Add the parboiled Rice to the stock and cook until the water is dried up.
  3. Preheat the oil in a pan, throw in the onions and fry for a minute or two, add the scallions, mixed vegetables, thyme, curry powder, salt, and stock Cube.
  4. Throw in the beef liver and rice. Stir-fry for about 3 to 5 Minutes.

And voila, you are done!
